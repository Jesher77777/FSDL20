DODGE SRT/DEMON


The "SRT Demon" refers to the Dodge Challenger SRT Demon, a high-performance muscle car known for its extreme acceleration and power, boasting a supercharged 6.2L V8 engine capable of producing over 1,000 horsepower, allowing it to reach 0-60 mph in under 2 seconds, making it one of the fastest production cars available; key features include a drag-focused suspension, special drag radial tires, a lightweight design achieved by removing unnecessary interior components, and a unique "Air Grabber" hood to maximize air intake for optimal cooling; the "SRT" stands for "Street and Racing Technology" signifying Dodge's high-performance division; the latest iteration is the "Demon 170" which further pushes the performance envelope with even more power and faster acceleration. Key points about the Srt demon

Engine:
Supercharged 6.2L HEMI V8 engine, producing over 1,000 horsepower on E85 fuel.

Performance:
0-60 mph in under 2 seconds, capable of running a quarter mile in the 8-second range.

Unique Features:
Drag-focused suspension Special drag radial tires "Air Grabber" hood Lightweight design with removed interior components Transbrake 2.0 system After-run chiller to cool the engine after high-performance runs

Latest Model:
The "Demon 170" with even higher horsepower and quicker acceleration
